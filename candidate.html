Shaik Rahamatunissa Begum

Born and raised in Machilipatnam , Andhrapradesh . Successfully accomplishing the education in Hindu High School in machilipatnam . While growing up she was determined to change and support the backward classes in Districts of Andhrapradesh.

The life and career of Shaik Rahamatunissa who choose to enter the male-driven and oftentimes exhausting path of politics have shown us that women are truly fearless. Rahamatunissa is not just a politician. She was a well recognised Arabic teacher , freely she led many muslim women to persue a formidable right of education through islamic region and language . Experiences upon such social reforming aspects she lead upliftment of Muslim minorities women , movements, and in 2021 she became the first female elected Counselor in 38th division of Vijayawada west constituency.

Her activism led her directly into politics where she became the face for Women and the Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party.By the guidence of her son Shri Hayat Shareef and she was selected to participate in the local body elections in conducting in 2021 ,by Shri Vellampalli Srinivasrao (legislator YSRCP) Andhrapradesh.She’s been a champion for gender equality all of her career and shows no plan to stop anytime soon

Her motherhood journey to surviving domestic conducts to becoming counselor is an extraordinary story of hope and resilience. She started Charity called 'Ismail Charities' established after the death of her Father in law Shri Shaik Ismail . Various Social service programs have been organised and executed through this charity to help the need and serving poor every year .Her journey from her humble beginning to running for Municipal Corporator is something to admire.
