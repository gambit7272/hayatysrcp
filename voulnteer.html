Why Join With Us?

The ward and village volunteer system that was unveiled on August 15 by Chief Minister Jaganmohan Reddy, is guided to help monitor and ensure deliverance of government welfare schemes to beneficiaries in the state. Each volunteer is supposed to access 50 households in the vicinity.Thanks to a network of village and ward volunteers which had put in place this year, the Andhra Pradesh government was able to smoothly execute its coronavirus containment measures and deliver social welfare schemes.

  • On 1st date of every month morning at 6'o clock, around 3 lakh ward and village volunteers spread into action door-to-door across Andhra Pradesh to deliver the monthly pensions to 59 lakh retired employees
  • On 1st date of every month morning at 6'o clock, around 3 lakh ward and village volunteers spread into action door-to-door across Andhra Pradesh to deliver the monthly pensions to 59 lakh retired employees
  • The volunteers are mostly in the age group of 18-35 years old, and resident of the locality. Each volunteer receives monthly salary of Rs 5000
  • The organisation of the village volunteers as part of the village secretariats has come in handy for the Reddy government during the Covid-19 pandemic.
  • According to CM Jagan Mohan Reddy, a total 2.16 lakh volunteers are assisting the ANMs, ASHA workers and other health workers on the Covid-19 duty. The volunteer system has now come in handy for the state government, which has been conducting large-scale surveys to identify and map covid-19 clusters and track nearly 14,000 people across the state
  • Looking at Volunteer system in A.p, We understand what volunteering is all us the people . Volunteers care so deeply, and they step up to support the things we believe in regardless of the personal cost. Thank you for being an inspiration to us all. We look forward to teaming with you again and continuing to make a difference together.
